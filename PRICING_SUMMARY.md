## 6. Key Profit Drivers

### High-Margin Elements
1. **Subscriptions (85-87% margin)** - Fixed monthly fee, predictable costs
2. **Top-up packs (90%+ margin)** - Emergency purchases at premium pricing
3. **Annual prepay (15% discount = 85% upfront cash)** - Reduces churn, improves cash flow

### Lower-Margin Elements (Strategic)
1. **Lead packs (37-58% margin)** - Volume discounts to encourage upgrades
2. **Call packs (50-69% margin)** - Engagement drivers, retention tools
