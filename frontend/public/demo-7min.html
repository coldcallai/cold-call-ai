7-Minute Killer Demo Script

THE RULE: No feature explanations. Only outcomes, pain, and forward motion.
This script doesn't win on information. It wins on momentum.

🎯 ASK BEFORE YOU START (Pick 2)

  • "Are you mostly inbound or outbound?"
  • "What's your biggest bottleneck—volume or conversion?"
  • "How are your reps handling tone mismatches on calls?"

Then tailor one line: "You said inbound is your bottleneck. Watch how this turns exactly those into closed deals."

⚠️ AUDIO PREP (Critical)

  • Pre-load call clip before demo starts
  • Test playback 3x — fumbling kills momentum
  • Have keyboard shortcut ready (spacebar to play)
  • Practice the transition: hook → "Let me play you..." → PLAY
0:00 – 0:30 THE HOOK (Pain + Question)
"Quick question—how many inbound or outbound leads are you missing or not converting right now?"
Let them answer. 5 seconds of silence if needed.
"Got it. Most reps treat every buyer the same. That's why deals die. Let me show you how we fix that in under 10 seconds."
0:30 – 1:30 🔥 LIVE CALL CLIP (Immediate Proof)
"First—let me play you a real example. This is from a live call last week."
Play 20-25 second clip where AI tone shifts mid-call

📞 EXAMPLE CALL TO PLAY (or read aloud)

AI: "Hi, this is Sarah calling from Acme Solutions. Do you have a quick moment?"
Prospect: "Yeah, but make it fast. I've got a meeting in 5." 🎯 D-TYPE DETECTED
AI: "Got it—I'll be direct. We help sales teams close 40% more deals by adapting to each buyer's style in real-time. Are you hitting your numbers this quarter or leaving money on the table?"
Prospect: "We're at about 85% of target."
AI: "That gap is exactly what we fix. I can get you a 15-minute walkthrough with a closer on our team. What's better—tomorrow at 2 or Thursday morning?"
Prospect: "Thursday works."
"What you just heard—that adjustment—is happening automatically in real-time. No training. No scripts. Just adaptation."
1:30 – 2:00 DISC (20 Seconds Max)
"There are four types of buyers. Some want speed. Some want trust. Some want data. Most reps talk to all of them the same way. That's why you're losing leads you already paid for."
"For example—that D-type buyer just heard our aggressive opener. An S-type hears the relationship version. Same AI. Different approach."
"We fix that."
Do NOT explain D-I-S-C individually. The call clips already proved it. Move on.
2:00 – 3:00 DASHBOARD (Outcomes Only)
Share screen → Dashboard
"This is where you control how your AI closes deals."
Point to Agents page
"Here—you set the tone for each buyer type. Aggressive for the fast ones. Patient for the trust-driven ones."
Point to Call History
"Every call gets analyzed here so you know exactly what's working and what's not. No guesswork."
Do NOT explain UI elements. Move quickly.
3:00 – 4:30 ⭐ LIVE TRANSFER (Your Gold)
"Here's where it gets unfair."
"Your rep gets on a live call. Before they speak, the AI already knows the buyer's style. It feeds them the right tone, the right pace, the right framing—in real time."
"Your reps walk into every call already knowing how to close that specific person."
Let that land for 2 seconds.
"That's not training. That's a superpower."
4:30 – 5:30 💰 ROI ANCHORING (No Fluff)
"Most teams using this go from losing 6 out of 10 warm leads to losing 4."
"That's two extra deals a week per rep."
What's two deals worth to you?
Let them answer. Do not fill the silence. They're doing the math.
5:30 – 6:15 PRICING (Only If They're Still In)
"Most of our customers see ROI within the first week."
"Plans start at $49/month for inbound, $149/month for outbound—depending on usage."
Do NOT list all tiers unless they ask.
If they hesitate on price: "We keep pricing low because we want usage, not headaches. You'll outgrow the $49 plan in two weeks anyway."
6:15 – 7:00 🎯 HARD CLOSE
"Let's get this running for you right now. I can set up your first campaign in 15 minutes."
STOP TALKING. Let them speak first.

⏸️ THREE PAUSE POINTS (Built In)

  1. After the hook — "How many leads are you missing?"
  2. After ROI anchoring — "What's two deals worth to you?"
  3. After the hard close — Silence. Let them speak first.

📞 ALTERNATE CALL EXAMPLE (S-Type / Relationship Buyer)

AI: "Hi, this is Sarah with Acme. Hope I'm not catching you at a bad time?"
Prospect: "No, it's okay. What's this about?" 🎯 S-TYPE DETECTED
AI: "I appreciate you taking a moment. I know you're busy. We work with sales teams to help them connect better with different types of buyers. I'm not here to pitch you—just wanted to see if it's something worth exploring together."
Prospect: "Okay, what do you mean by different types?"
AI: "Great question. Some buyers want the facts fast. Others—like yourself—prefer to build a bit of trust first. Most sales reps miss that. We help them adapt. Would you be open to a quick call with someone on our team who can walk you through it? No pressure at all."
Prospect: "Sure, that sounds reasonable."
