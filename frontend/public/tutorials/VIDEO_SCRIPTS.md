## VIDEO 2: Setting Up AI Agents (4:15)

**[SHOW: Agents page]**

Let's dive deeper into creating the perfect AI agent. Your agent is basically your virtual salesperson — so let's make them great.

**[SHOW: Click Create Agent]**

Click Create Agent to start fresh.

**[SHOW: Agent name field]**

First, the name. This is internal — your leads won't see it. Use something descriptive like "SaaS Outreach Agent" or "Restaurant Qualifier."

**[SHOW: Use case / industry dropdown if available]**

If you're targeting a specific industry, select it here. This helps the AI understand context and speak the right language.

**[SHOW: Voice selection area]**

Now the fun part — the voice. You've got two options.

**[SHOW: Preset voices grid]**

Option one: Preset voices. These are professional ElevenLabs voices ready to go. Rachel is calm and professional. Josh is friendly and energetic. Pick one that matches your brand.

**[SHOW: Clone voice button]**

Option two: Clone your voice. Upload one to five audio samples — at least 30 seconds total — and we'll create a custom voice that sounds like you. Or your best salesperson. Your choice.

**[SHOW: Voice settings gear icon → modal opens]**

Click the gear icon to fine-tune your voice. You'll see three sliders.

Stability controls consistency. Lower means more expressive and human. Higher means more robotic but predictable. For sales calls, I recommend point-four to point-six.

Similarity is how close to the original voice. Keep this around point-seven-five for clarity.

Style is energy level. Low is calm, high is enthusiastic. Match it to your campaign type.

**[SHOW: Preview button being clicked]**

Always click Preview before saving. Hear exactly how your agent will sound.

**[SHOW: Script/prompt area]**

Finally, your script. This is what your AI says. Write naturally — use contractions, add pauses with dot-dot-dot, and keep sentences short. The more human it reads, the more human it sounds.

**[SHOW: Save button]**

Save your agent, and you're ready to add them to a campaign!

## VIDEO 3: Voice Tuning Masterclass (3:45)

**[SHOW: Voice Settings modal]**

Your AI's voice can make or break a call. Let's master the voice settings.

**[SHOW: Quick presets section]**

First, the easy way — Quick Presets. We've got three ready to go.

**[SHOW: Click Professional preset]**

Professional is calm and trustworthy. Perfect for B2B sales, consultants, and enterprise deals.

**[SHOW: Click Conversational preset]**

Conversational is natural and friendly. Great for warm leads or follow-ups.

**[SHOW: Click Energetic preset]**

Energetic is upbeat and enthusiastic. Use this for promotions, events, or high-energy offers.

**[SHOW: Sliders section]**

Now let's go manual.

**[SHOW: Stability slider]**

Stability. This is the most important one. 

At zero-point-three, your AI sounds very human — expressive, with natural variation. But it might sound slightly different each call.

At zero-point-eight, it's consistent and predictable. But also more robotic.

The sweet spot for sales? Zero-point-four to zero-point-five-five. Human enough to connect, stable enough to trust.

**[SHOW: Similarity slider]**

Similarity. This is how close to the original voice sample.

Keep it between zero-point-seven and zero-point-eight. Too low and the voice sounds muddy. Too high and it can sound artificial.

**[SHOW: Style slider]**

Style. This is your energy dial.

Zero-point-two is calm and measured — good for serious topics.

Zero-point-five is balanced — works for most cases.

Zero-point-seven-plus is animated and enthusiastic — great for promotions.

**[SHOW: Preview button]**

Here's the golden rule: Always preview. Click this button, listen, adjust, repeat. Your ears are the final judge.

**[SHOW: Closing the modal]**

Once it sounds right, hit Save. Your agent is now perfectly tuned.
